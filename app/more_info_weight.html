Official Rules and Conditions
How to Start?

Initial Weigh-Ins: The competition begins at noon on

Sundays. Participants will have till Monday noon to

weigh-in and show proof of their current weight to

begin participating. This initial weigh-in will establish a

player’s official starting weight.


Duration/Final Weigh-In: All games are 14 days long and the final weigh-in

will be due on the 3rd Sunday at midnight.All players will have to show proof

of their ending weight to be qualified.


Photo – Based Proof of Weight: Each weigh-in must be provided by two

photos to be verified. The first photo will be a head-to-toe shot of the player

standing on the scale wearing lightweight clothing (explained in Dress Code).

The second must be a photo of the scale indicating the player’s current weight

with a piece of paper showing the time of upload to ensure the weigh-in was

done at the appropriate time.


How to Win?

Bet Payments: Bets for the games must be paid

up-front. The bet amount for each game is set

by the organizer, and you can pay via PayPal,

credit card,or with your winnings from another

game.


There will be an Administration Processing Fee with every placed

bet. All bets will be collected by the Administration and kept until

the payouts are initiated.


Winning: The top three players with the most steps for that week

will be become the winners. All winners will be announced by mid-

night on Monday.


Payouts: 1st place winner will earn 50% of the pot. 2nd place will

earn 30% of the pot and the 3rd place will earn 20% of the pot. For

example, if the total pot is $100 the first place will earn $50, second

place will earn $30 and the third place will earn $20.

What to know?

Privacy: All players weigh-ins and photo proofs will be kept

confidential and private unless the player decides to update

their weight throughout the week.







All photos are only seen by the Administration in order to determine

the winners. Photos will not be used for any other purpose.


Dress Code: Weigh-In have a dress code as stated above. Each player must wear

lightweight clothings and equivalent clothings for both official weigh-ins. This will

ensure that the number shown on the scale is accurate and not fluctuated due to

heavy clothing.


Scales: Similar or the same scale should be used for both official weigh-ins. Scales

should be placed on a hard and flat surface. Scales should be either digital or doc-

tor’s scale

Unofficial weigh-ins: All players are recommended to weigh in twice a

week to keep track of their progress and improvements.


Kilograms, Pounds: Players must choose to weigh in kilograms or pounds.

All units will be harmonized before determining the winner.


Simultaneous Games: Players must join and finish one Weight-Off game at

a time before moving on to the next. Participants are allowed to play

Wieght-Off and Step-On together at any time.


Age Requirement: All participants must be 18 years or older


Participation: Everyone is welcome to participate in any competitions

during their 10-day free trial but need a membership to continue with any

competitions or challenges.


Refunds: Once a bet is placed there will be no refunds.


Disclaimer and Indemnification

Be Aware of the Health Risks: Please consider your health

and consult with your primary physician before engaging in

any physical activities.


Be Smart: Do not engage in any risky behavior that will cause

you to lose an extensive amount of weight in a short period of

time.


Health Notification: Participate only if your health permits

this activity. Consult a qualified healthcare provider before

participating should you have any health issues that could be

impacted by the activity required for this game.
